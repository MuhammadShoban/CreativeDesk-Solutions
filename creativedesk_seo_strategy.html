⚠️ Critical Issue: You're on a Vercel Subdomain

Your site is hosted on creative-desk-solutions.vercel.app — a free Vercel subdomain. Google sees the domain authority of the entire vercel.app subdomain, not just your page. This severely hurts your ability to rank. To rank for competitive keywords, you MUST move to a custom domain like creativedesksolutions.com. A custom domain costs ~$10/year and will 5x your SEO potential. This is the single most important step.

Great content attracts backlinks automatically — these topics will rank AND get shared

People Google this constantly. Write a detailed guide with price ranges. This alone can rank on page 1 and attract 5+ natural backlinks per month.

High-volume tech comparison. Easy to rank long-term. Developers will link to it from forums and communities.

Real case studies build trust AND attract links from business blogs. Prospective clients share these. Name the client if they allow it.

Targets people searching to hire, not just research. Very high buying intent. Include pricing, process, what to ask. This will rank for tons of long-tail terms.

Educational content for SMEs. Gets linked from business blogs, Facebook groups, and YouTube descriptions. High shareability.

Local + trend content. Other Pakistani business blogs will link to it. Works great for local SEO and social shares.
